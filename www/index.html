ABOUT | RESUME

Jason C Lee is a designer who graduated with a BFA in Graphic Design and a minor in Print Media from University of Maryland, baltimore County in May 2013. After graduated from college, Jason is currently working as a graphic designer for Posterity Group LLC. His duties were required to interpret clients' idea visually, and developed concepts, graphics and layouts for print productions, animation, re-branding the company logo, and re-established the company's website by using the skills in Adobe softwares from Adobe Creative Cloud. In the meantime, he also provided a basic technical support to IT administrator as required. He has also been the graphic designer for Election Board in 2013, a branch in the Student Government Association (SGA) of UMBC. He had a pleasure of working in team to re-brand the branch logo, a variety of marketing materials, and web advertising. Jason also has experience volunteering in a VisArt as a gallery intern. He was able to create a variety of print marketing materials managing volunteers for general office work, and gallery installation of exhibition shows.
